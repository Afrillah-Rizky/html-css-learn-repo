About

Welcome to Mangata & Gallo

At Mangata & Gallo, we believe in the timeless allure of fine jewelry, crafted with passion and precision. Our name, inspired by the Swedish word for the shimmering, magical trail of moonlight on water, reflects our commitment to creating pieces that capture the essence of beauty and elegance.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to redefine luxury jewelry, Mangata & Gallo brings together a legacy of craftsmanship and contemporary design. Each piece in our collection tells a story of artistry and dedication, crafted by skilled artisans who blend traditional techniques with modern aesthetics.

Our Collections

Explore our exclusive collections, curated to appeal to every style and occasion. From exquisite diamond engagement rings that symbolize eternal love to statement necklaces that elevate any ensemble, Mangata & Gallo offers jewelry that resonates with sophistication and grace.

Craftsmanship

We take pride in our commitment to quality and detail. Every piece undergoes meticulous craftsmanship and is created using the finest materials, ensuring not only beauty but also longevity. Whether you seek a personalized creation or a cherished gift, our artisans are dedicated to exceeding your expectations.
